ABOUT KERALA

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is known for its natural beauty, serene backwaters, lush green landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It is often referred to as "God's Own Country."

With its stunning beaches, tranquil hill stations, and vibrant wildlife sanctuaries, Kerala offers a diverse range of attractions. The famous backwaters, such as Alleppey and Kumarakom, provide a unique experience of cruising on traditional houseboats known as "kettuvallams."

Kerala is also renowned for its Ayurvedic treatments and therapies, which attract visitors from all around the world. The state's cuisine is flavorful and includes dishes like appam, puttu, and seafood specialties.

Additionally, Kerala showcases a rich cultural heritage with traditional art forms like Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, and Theyyam. The state also celebrates several festivals, including Onam, Vishu, and Thrissur Pooram, with great enthusiasm.
